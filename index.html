Projects and Internships

    [1]   GAN-based magic pen Ma Liang cartoon picture production

    • Project description: Artificial intelligence is more and more widely used in life, and the scenes in many movies continue to become a reality. In particular, the great progress of the generation confrontation network (GAN), whether writing poetry, music or painting, has confused the false with the true, and even exceeded the level of human beings. Can AI also turn our childhood fairy tales into reality and turn children's or adults' graffiti into exquisite cartoons, paintings of other styles, or even 3D animation? Based on the idea of generation against network in deep learning, this project can turn the user's graffiti into a number of exquisite cartoons with rich colors and different styles.
    • Duration: 2019.7-2019.9
    • Place: Deecamp2019, Held at the University of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
    • Role: Team member

    [2]   Data validity verification in video HDR task

    • Project description: Live HDR is mainly used to solve the inconsistency between preview and live shooting. Preview is the image seen in the lens, and real shooting is the image taken. Because the paths of preview stream and photo stream are different, there are great differences between them, mainly reflected in the brightening effect of dark area, the restoration of details in high exposure area, the brightness of night scene (preview is much worse than real shooting results), etc. The project aims to improve the consistency between preview and real shooting from the perspective of data effectiveness.
    • Duration: 2021.5-2023.4
    • Place: Huawei Nanjing R&D Center in the early stage and NUAA in the later stage
    • Role: Team leader
